ABOUT ME

I'm a Yale University class of 2024 undergraduate majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive Science. I was born and brought up in Singapore. I have always been an active child, and from a young age heavily involved in sports. At the age of 8, i picked up Squash and soon after at the age of 11, became the youngest member of the Singapore National Team. Since then, i have been a student athlete, juggling sport and studies and have thoroughly enjoyed the journey. It has brought me all over the world, instilled values like perservere,grit, determination and valuable skills like time management and have learnt the art of decision making.
